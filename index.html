the shift

The client journey changed quietly.

Referrals still start most engagements — that hasn't changed. What changed is what happens next. Before a client calls, they check. And increasingly that check happens inside an AI assistant, which answers not with ten links but with two or three specific firm names. The firms it names win the introduction. The ones it leaves out lose it, silently, without ever knowing they were considered.

Ranking on Google and being cited by AI used to be the same thing. They have come apart. In mid-2025, 76% of Google's AI answer citations came from pages already ranking in the top 10. By early 2026, that was 38%. — Ahrefs, 2026.
